Due dates on assessments

Why the due dates on assessments should be staggered

Current Problems

  • Students have to deal with massive waves of assessments each year all due in waves of 3-6, which builds tones of stress, and having to switch between multiple assessments, all which makes focusing hard.
  • If students are unable to focus, it means that the time it takes to complete a assessment will increase, as there are more assessments each year, and less time to do them, which all cause grades to go down.

What is the effect of the change

  • Spread out due dates means that students can focus on a singular assessment at a time, rather than having to submit 4 or 5 different assessments at the same time.
  • If due dates are spread out, Students get to have more time with their parents, as students can complete their assessments faster with more time on a single one

How to make a change?

If you want to make a change, please spread the word, and make your self be heard, our grades are in you hands.
